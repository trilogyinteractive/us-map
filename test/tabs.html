League of Conservation Voters Our Election Impact

The League of Conservation Voters is poised to have our biggest election cycle ever, and play a decisive role in shaping the political landscape in Washington. In 2011, LCV outlined a clear strategy for the cycle of focusing our resources in fewer places to have a disproportionate impact on key races.

National

For decades, LCV’s signature electoral program has been the Dirty Dozen, which targets the 12 most anti-environmental candidates in competitive races. This cycle we invested more resources in the program than ever before—spending more than $10 million in Dirty Dozen races alone—and placed a greater emphasis on the issue of climate change by adding the Flat Earth Five to the list. In the end, we had our most successful program ever by defeating 11 out of 12.
